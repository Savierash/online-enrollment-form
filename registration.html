Official Enrollment Process

Marketing Department

  1. Fill up Student Permanent Record (SPR) & Info Sheet
  2. Verification of Required Documents (if available):
    • PSA Birth Certificate
    • High School/Senior High School Report Card (F138)
    • Form 137 (may follow)

Registrar and Finance

  1. Encoding of SPR
  2. Admission
  3. Submission of Requirement (if available):
    • PSA Birth Certificate
    • High School/Senior High School Report Card (F138)
    • Form 137 (may follow)
  4. Downpayment
    • College Non-scholars: can pay P1,000.00 for registration fee
    • College Scholars: can pay P750.00 for registration fee
    • SHS: can pay P500.00 for registration fee
  5. Issuance of Official Receipt (OR) of down payment.

Marketing Department

  1. Verification of issued OR number
  2. Printing of ORF (Official Registration Form) or COM (Certificate of Matriculation)
  3. Join designated Facebook groups and fill up ID Application Form
